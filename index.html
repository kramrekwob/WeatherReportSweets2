Who I Am

Jonny Kaul moved to Vermont in 2011 to pursue a degree in Engineering at UVM with one of my major incentives being Vermont’s progressive views on Cannabis. While in college I worked on recipe development and dosing control. My skills from engineering school have allowed me to develop extrapolation techniques that have given me the skills to develop basic solubility properties of cannabis into various fat gobules. In my 10 years of living in Vermont I have been active in the Lamoille county and chittenden county communities and built fortuitous connections. I’ve worked in the farming and beer industries and have learned a copious amount of information about food product processing.

Our Mission

Weather Report Sweets strives to empower the consumer through offering a range of delicious and creative cookie styles for everyone and every kind of collective cookie experience. Our aim is to promote and reinvest in the Vermont cannabis community, using locally sourced and legally grown cannabis. Our mission is deeply rooted in having a positive impact in our community through socially and environmentally conscious practice, and creating a cookie for all.
